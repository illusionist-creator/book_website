WHY THIS

I made this because I want to appreciate you.

You are the reason this exists — every pixel, every word.

I don’t think I can ever fully explain how much you mean to me.

Even this won’t do you complete justice… but this is the least I can do.

Every line here is a small attempt to capture what you mean to me.

“Some people come into our lives and leave footprints on our hearts.”

ADMIRATION

I admire you more than I could ever put into words.

You are a masterpiece I never knew I needed.

The way you exist makes the world better place to be in somehow.

Your simplest touch calms my hardest storms. (And that’s not an exaggeration.)

Talking to you feels like the solution to every problem — even when you’re the problem. (In the best way possible.)

You let me be who I am — no filters, no masks, just me.

You made me realize that sometimes we don’t just miss memories… we miss people.

Your presence matter, more then you know or more then I know.

WHAT MAKES YOU, YOU

It’s the little things.

The way you care about your hair like it’s a full-time responsibility, no days off.

The way you dance after you have your favorite food.

Sometimes I think, I can read you like a book. But deep down you are the mystery no one truly knows.

The way you passionately rant about people you dislike.

The way your voice softens when you talk about the people you love.

The way you make everyone feel at home around you.

I could go on and on… and still, no one does it better than you.

Sometimes I feel like you are the most misunderstood character in everyone else’s story. And honestly, I feel bad for the ones who fail to see you the way you deserve to be seen.

You are rare. And not everyone recognizes rare things when they see them.

Your laugh, your quirks, your silences — they all paint a picture only a few get to admire.

THE PLACE YOU HOLD

I’ve met many people in my life.

But you didn’t just become another person in it — you became a part of it.

You’re the person I want to tell things to first. The one whose opinion matters. The one whose mood affects mine more than I admit.

You became someone my days quietly revolve around. (I genuinely miss you when you are not around).

Morning rides with you aren’t just rides. They are best and something I look forward to every single day.

I genuinely feel like I wouldn’t need weekends if every morning it was us our way.

And I truly believe this — you’re the only person I would listen to, even when I’m really mad.

Life feels better when you are around.

Every moment with you is a memory I hold very close, a treasure I never take for granted.

APOLOGIES

I know you HATE the word “sorry.”

But some things need to be said.

For the times I wasn’t my best, for the moments I caused you pain.

But after everything you’ve done for me — even the lies you had to tell just to keep me out of trouble — it only shows how incredible of a human being you are.

No matter what people think or say about you, the ones who truly care about you will always stand by your side.

And you are one of those rare people.

I’m sorry for every single thing you had to go through because of me.

You've been my anchor, even when I didn't realize it. I’m sorry I didn’t always see it.

MY ASKS

(I am a man, after all.)

Morning and evening rides with you. Just you and me, the road, and the world fading away.

Hold my hand occasionally. It quiets my entire mind. That’s your superpower.

I know I’ve said this more times than I can count… but keep walking, Yashvi. Even if there’s a dead end ahead, I wouldn’t mind sitting there in silence forever — as long as it’s with you.

And last but never least — stay happy. Always. No matter what anyone thinks or says about you. You are one of the most courageous, intelligent, and beautiful girls I have ever met.

You are the best sesh, desert, exploration partner I could ever have and I do not intend it to change, its either you or no-one.

Thank you for being you & Thank you for staying. It means more than I could ever say.

Thank You
For everything.
